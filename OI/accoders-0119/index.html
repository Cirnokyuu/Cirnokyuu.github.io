题意

题解

fi,min⁡(j,k)←fi,j′+fson,k(j+k−2di≤K)f_{i,\min(j,k)} \leftarrow f'_{i,j}+f_{son,k} (j+k-2d_i \leq K) fi,min(j,k)​←fi,j′​+fson,k​(j+k−2di​≤K)
